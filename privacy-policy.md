# Caption Goblin Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

Caption Goblin and Caption Goblin Premium ("the Apps") are developed by Euphoria Creations.

## Information We Collect

Caption Goblin and Caption Goblin Premium do not collect, store, or transmit personal information to Euphoria Creations.

Any text entered into the apps is processed locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

## Settings and Preferences

The apps store user preferences locally on your device. These settings may include formatting options, custom filler word lists, saved presets, and other app preferences.

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to Euphoria Creations.

## Advertising

Caption Goblin (Free) uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Caption Goblin Premium does not display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and process data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and advertising policies. Users should review Google's Privacy Policy for additional information about how advertising data may be collected and used.

## External Links

The apps may provide links to external websites, such as Ko-fi or other official Euphoria Creations pages. Euphoria Creations is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

## Children's Privacy

The apps are not directed toward children under the age of 13.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
