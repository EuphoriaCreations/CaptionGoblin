Caption Goblin Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Caption Goblin is developed by Euphoria Creations.

Information We Collect

Caption Goblin does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to Euphoria Creations.

Any text entered into the app is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Settings and Preferences

The app stores user preferences locally on your device. These settings may include formatting options and custom filler word lists used by the app.

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to Euphoria Creations.

Advertising

Caption Goblin uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and process data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and advertising policies.

External Links

The app may provide links to external websites, such as Ko-fi.

Children's Privacy

Caption Goblin is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
